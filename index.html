ROMA Lab

ROMA Lab (Responsible Online Media Analytics Lab) explores the frontier between AI and Social Media, aiming to build Next-Generation AI for Social Science. Guided by the principles of being Green, Human-Centric, and Trustworthy, we pursue research that is theoretically profound with real-world impact — empowering AI to truly understand and serve human society.
